Corny Cornbread Corn Casserole

Ingredients

1 cancream-style corn
1 canwhole kernel corn
2eggs, beaten
1 stickmargarine, cut up
1 cupsour cream
4 tablespoonsonion, chopped
2 tablespoonsgreen pepper, chopped
1 boxJiffy Corn Bread mix
¼ teaspoonsalt
¼ teaspoonpepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Grease a 2qt casserole dish
  3. Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl
  4. Pour mixture into the casserole dish
  5. Bake for 45 minutes, or until top is golden brown
  6. Let stand for 5 minues. Enjoy!
